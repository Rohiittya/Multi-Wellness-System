Vegetarian Muscle Gain Recipes

1. Tofu and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced tofu and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add the bell pepper, broccoli florets, and snap peas. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Season with salt and pepper and serve.

2. Chickpea and Spinach Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 4 cups spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and curry powder. Cook for another minute.
  3. Add the chickpeas and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

3. Quinoa and Black Bean Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a medium saucepan, combine quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes or until water is absorbed.
  2. Let the quinoa cool, then combine it with black beans, diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro.
  3. Drizzle with lime juice and season with salt and pepper. Toss to combine and serve.

4. Lentil and Sweet Potato Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups lentils, cooked
  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil sweet potatoes until tender, then mash and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  4. Add carrots, peas, lentils, broth, thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer to a baking dish, top with mashed sweet potatoes. Bake for 20-25 minutes.

5. Black Bean and Corn Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Small corn tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine black beans, corn, bell pepper, red onion, olive oil, lime juice, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Warm tortillas in a skillet.
  3. Fill each tortilla with the bean mixture, top with avocado slices, and garnish with cilantro.

6. Spinach and Feta Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers, halved and seeded
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until softened. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. In a bowl, mix cooked quinoa, sautéed spinach, and feta cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Fill each bell pepper half with the mixture and place in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender.

7. Baked Falafel with Tahini Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, parsley, cilantro, onion, garlic, cumin, coriander, baking powder, flour, salt, and pepper. Pulse until well combined but still chunky.
  3. Shape the mixture into small patties and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Brush the patties with olive oil and bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. Serve with tahini sauce.

8. Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and brush with olive oil. Place on a baking sheet, gill side up.
  3. In a bowl, mix spinach, ricotta, Parmesan, cherry tomatoes, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff each mushroom with the mixture and bake for 20-25 minutes.

9. Tempeh and Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 block tempeh, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, olive oil, maple syrup, garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate tempeh cubes in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread tempeh, bell pepper, zucchini, red onion, and cherry tomatoes onto skewers.
  5. Grill skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally.

10. Vegan Lentil Loaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils, cooked
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup carrots, grated
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp flaxseed meal
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a loaf pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  4. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

11. Spicy Peanut Tofu Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups brown rice, cooked
  • 1 cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, chopped
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, and sriracha.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, cook tofu until golden brown, and coat with the peanut sauce.
  3. Assemble bowls with brown rice, tofu, red cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, and avocado. Top with chopped peanuts.

12. Cauliflower and Chickpea Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Small corn tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss cauliflower and chickpeas with olive oil, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Warm tortillas in a skillet.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the roasted cauliflower and chickpeas. Garnish with cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
