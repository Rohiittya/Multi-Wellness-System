Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill for 6-8 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  3. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice it thinly.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  5. Top with grilled chicken slices and crumbled feta cheese.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Toss gently to combine.

Baked Salmon with Asparagus

Ingredients:
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets and asparagus on a baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Top with lemon slices.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the salmon is cooked through and the asparagus is tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

Turkey Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 head of lettuce, leaves separated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and garlic, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add ground turkey and cook until browned.
  4. Add diced bell pepper, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and grated ginger. Cook for a few more minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the turkey mixture in lettuce leaves as wraps.

Shrimp and Avocado Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque.
  3. In a large bowl, combine diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  4. Add cooked shrimp and toss gently to combine.
  5. Drizzle with lime juice and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Chicken and Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced chicken breasts and cook until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Add the broccoli florets, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and chicken broth. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
  6. Toss everything to coat evenly with the sauce. Cook for a couple more minutes until heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.

Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
Procedure:
  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced flank steak and cook until browned.
  5. Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for a few minutes until tender-crisp.
  6. Pour the sauce mixture over the beef and broccoli. Stir well to coat.
  7. Add beef broth and cook for a couple more minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.
