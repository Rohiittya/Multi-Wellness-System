Overall Wellness Breakfast Recepies

Oatmeal with Berries

A warm and comforting bowl of oatmeal topped with fresh berries and honey.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup rolled oats
  • - 2 cups water or milk
  • - 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • - 1 tbsp honey
  • - 1/4 cup almonds, sliced
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a pot, bring water or milk to a boil.
  • 2. Add rolled oats and reduce heat to a simmer.
  • 3. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • 4. Serve topped with mixed berries, honey, and sliced almonds.
Avocado Toast

A simple and healthy breakfast option with mashed avocado on toast, topped with a sprinkle of salt and pepper.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 ripe avocado
  • - 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • - Optional: cherry tomatoes, radishes, or poached egg
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Toast the slices of bread to your desired level of crispiness.
  • 2. Mash the ripe avocado in a bowl.
  • 3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on each toast slice.
  • 4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • 5. Optional: Top with cherry tomatoes, radishes, or a poached egg.
Smoothie Bowl

A vibrant and nutritious smoothie bowl topped with fresh fruit, granola, and chia seeds.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup frozen berries
  • - 1 banana
  • - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • - 1/2 cup almond milk
  • - Toppings: fresh fruit, granola, chia seeds
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Blend the frozen berries, banana, Greek yogurt, and almond milk until smooth.
  • 2. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  • 3. Top with fresh fruit, granola, and chia seeds.
Greek Yogurt Parfait

A creamy and delicious parfait layered with Greek yogurt, honey, granola, and fresh fruit.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • - 2 tbsp honey
  • - 1/2 cup granola
  • - 1 cup mixed fresh fruit
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt, honey, granola, and fresh fruit.
  • 2. Repeat layers as desired.
  • 3. Serve immediately.
Vegetable Omelette

A fluffy omelette loaded with fresh vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and spinach.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 3 eggs
  • - 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • - 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • - 1/4 cup spinach leaves
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  • 2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  • 3. Add bell peppers and onions, and sauté until softened.
  • 4. Add spinach leaves and cook until wilted.
  • 5. Serve hot.
Chia Pudding

A creamy chia pudding made with almond milk, topped with fresh fruits and nuts.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • - 1 cup almond milk
  • - 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • - Toppings: fresh fruits, nuts
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, mix chia seeds, almond milk, and maple syrup.
  • 2. Let it sit in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • 3. Stir well before serving and top with fresh fruits and nuts.
Breakfast Burrito

A hearty breakfast burrito filled with scrambled eggs, black beans, cheese, and salsa.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 large eggs
  • - 1/4 cup black beans, rinsed
  • - 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • - 2 tbsp salsa
  • - 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Scramble the eggs in a skillet over medium heat.
  • 2. Warm the tortilla in a separate pan.
  • 3. Place scrambled eggs, black beans, shredded cheese, and salsa in the tortilla.
  • 4. Roll the tortilla into a burrito and serve warm.
Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes served with butter and maple syrup.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup flour
  • - 1 tbsp sugar
  • - 1 tsp baking powder
  • - 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • - 1 cup buttermilk
  • - 1 egg
  • - 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  • 2. Add buttermilk, egg, and melted butter, and mix until smooth.
  • 3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes.
  • 4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  • 5. Serve with butter and maple syrup.
Fruit Salad

A refreshing mix of seasonal fruits, topped with a squeeze of lime.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup watermelon, diced
  • - 1 cup pineapple, diced
  • - 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • - 1 kiwi, sliced
  • - 1 lime, juiced
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a large bowl, combine all the diced fruits.
  • 2. Squeeze lime juice over the fruit and toss gently to mix.
  • 3. Serve immediately or chill before serving.
French Toast

Classic French toast made with thick slices of bread, eggs, and cinnamon.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 4 slices of thick bread
  • - 2 large eggs
  • - 1/2 cup milk
  • - 1 tsp cinnamon
  • - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • - Butter for cooking
  • - Maple syrup and powdered sugar for topping
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
  • 2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, ensuring both sides are coated.
  • 3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and melt butter.
  • 4. Cook the bread slices until golden brown on both sides.
  • 5. Serve with maple syrup and powdered sugar.
Egg Muffins

Baked egg muffins with vegetables and cheese, perfect for a grab-and-go breakfast.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 6 large eggs
  • - 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • - 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • - 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a muffin tin.
  • 2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Add diced bell peppers, chopped spinach, and shredded cheese.
  • 4. Pour the mixture into the muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
  • 5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the eggs are fully cooked.
  • 6. Let cool before removing from the tin.
Granola with Yogurt

Crunchy granola served with creamy Greek yogurt and a drizzle of honey.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup granola
  • - 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • - 1 tbsp honey
  • - Optional: fresh fruits or nuts for topping
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, layer Greek yogurt and granola.
  • 2. Drizzle honey on top.
  • 3. Add fresh fruits or nuts if desired.
  • 4. Serve immediately.
