Grilled Chicken and Brown Rice

Ingredients:
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 cup cooked brown rice
- Steamed broccoli
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.
2. Slice the chicken and serve over cooked brown rice.
3. Add steamed broccoli on the side.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve and enjoy.

Greek Chicken Power Bowl

Ingredients:
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- Chopped cucumber, tomatoes, and olives
- 1/4 cup feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.
2. Slice the chicken and serve over cooked quinoa.
3. Add chopped cucumber, tomatoes, and olives.
4. Sprinkle with feta cheese.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve and enjoy.

Pesto Chicken and Whole Wheat Pasta

Ingredients:
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 cup whole wheat pasta
- 2 tablespoons pesto sauce
- Cherry tomatoes
- Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Cook the whole wheat pasta according to package instructions.
2. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.
3. Slice the chicken and toss with cooked pasta and pesto sauce.
4. Add cherry tomatoes.
5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve and enjoy.

Chickpea and Spinach Salad

Ingredients:
- 1 cup cooked chickpeas
- 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
- 1/4 cup diced red onions
- 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. In a large bowl, combine cooked chickpeas, fresh spinach, red onions, and bell peppers.
2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Toss well and serve.

Egg White Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:
- 4 egg whites
- 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
- 1/4 cup diced onions
- 1/4 cup shredded cheese
- Whole wheat tortilla
- Salsa
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Cook the egg whites in a pan with diced bell peppers and onions.
2. Place the cooked egg whites in a whole wheat tortilla.
3. Add shredded cheese and salsa.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Roll up the tortilla and serve.

Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients:
- 2 sweet potatoes
- 1/2 cup black beans
- 1/2 cup corn kernels
- 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
- 1/4 cup shredded cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Bake or microwave the sweet potatoes until tender.
2. Cut the sweet potatoes in half and scoop out some of the flesh.
3. In a bowl, combine black beans, corn, and bell peppers.
4. Stuff the sweet potatoes with the black bean mixture.
5. Sprinkle with shredded cheese and drizzle with olive oil.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve and enjoy.

Salmon and Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:
- 1 salmon fillet
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- Mixed salad greens
- 1/4 cup diced cucumber
- 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Grill or bake the salmon fillet until fully cooked.
2. In a bowl, combine cooked quinoa, mixed salad greens, cucumber, and tomatoes.
3. Flake the cooked salmon and add it to the bowl.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Toss well and serve.

Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 200g lean beef strips
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan and stir-fry the beef strips until browned.
2. Add broccoli and bell peppers to the pan.
3. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
4. Drizzle with soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve and enjoy.

Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:
- 1 chicken breast
- 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
- 1/2 cup sliced onions
- Whole wheat tortillas
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Fajita seasoning
- Salsa
- Salt and pepper to taste

Process:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the chicken breast until fully cooked.
2. Slice the chicken and set aside.
3. In the same pan, cook bell peppers and onions until tender.
4. Add fajita seasoning and mix well.
5. Place the cooked chicken and vegetables in whole wheat tortillas.
6. Add salsa and season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve and enjoy.
