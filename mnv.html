Grilled Chicken and Quinoa Bowl

Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions using water or chicken broth.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill for 6-8 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice it thinly.
  5. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  6. Top with grilled chicken slices and crumbled feta cheese.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Toss gently to combine.

Herb-Crusted Salmon

Ingredients:
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a small bowl, combine breadcrumbs, parsley, thyme, and lemon zest.
  5. Press the breadcrumb mixture onto the top of the salmon fillets.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and the topping is golden brown.
  7. Serve with lemon wedges.

Greek Turkey Meatballs

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground turkey, breadcrumbs, feta cheese, minced garlic, parsley, dill, egg, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix well and form into meatballs.
  4. Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the meatballs are cooked through and golden brown.
  6. Serve with a side of tzatziki sauce if desired.

Shrimp and Avocado Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque.
  3. In a large bowl, combine diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  4. Add cooked shrimp and toss gently to combine.
  5. Drizzle with lime juice and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Chicken and Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced chicken breasts and cook until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Add the broccoli florets, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and chicken broth. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
  6. Toss everything to coat evenly with the sauce. Cook for a couple more minutes until heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.

Tuna Salad Wrap

Ingredients:
  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole wheat wraps
  • Mixed greens for serving
Procedure:
  1. In a large bowl, combine tuna, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, celery, red onion, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until well combined.
  3. Lay out a whole wheat wrap and add a layer of mixed greens.
  4. Top with a generous scoop of tuna salad.
  5. Roll up the wrap tightly and slice in half before serving.
