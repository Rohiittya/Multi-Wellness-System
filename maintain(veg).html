Vegetarian Weight Maintenance Recipes

Quinoa and Black Bean Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked and drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions using water or vegetable broth.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, black beans, red bell pepper, and red onion.
  3. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice. Toss gently to combine.

Herb-Crusted Tofu

Ingredients:
  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place tofu cubes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a small bowl, combine breadcrumbs, parsley, thyme, and lemon zest.
  5. Press the breadcrumb mixture onto the tofu cubes.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the tofu is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve with lemon wedges.

Greek Chickpea Patties

Ingredients:
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine mashed chickpeas, breadcrumbs, feta cheese, minced garlic, parsley, dill, egg, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix well and form into patties.
  4. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the patties are golden brown.
  6. Serve with a side of tzatziki sauce if desired.

Avocado and Edamame Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup edamame, shelled and cooked
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Procedure:
  1. In a large bowl, combine edamame, diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and vegetable broth. Pour the sauce over the vegetables.
  5. Toss everything to coat evenly with the sauce. Cook for a couple more minutes until heated through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.

Caprese Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:
  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella, cubed
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the portobello mushrooms on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil. Stuff the mixture into the portobello mushrooms.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes or until the mushrooms are tender and the cheese is melted.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.
