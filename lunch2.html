Grilled Chicken Salad

A hearty salad with grilled chicken, fresh vegetables, and a light dressing.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g grilled chicken breast
  • - 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • - 1/4 cup red onions, sliced
  • - 1/4 cup cucumbers, sliced
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Grill the chicken breast and slice into strips.
  • 2. Mix the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onions, and cucumbers in a bowl.
  • 3. Top with grilled chicken.
  • 4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  • 5. Season with salt and pepper.
Quinoa and Black Bean Bowl

A protein-packed quinoa bowl with black beans, corn, and avocado.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • - 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • - 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • - 1 avocado, diced
  • - 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • - 1 lime, juiced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions.
  • 2. In a bowl, combine quinoa, black beans, corn, and avocado.
  • 3. Add chopped cilantro and lime juice.
  • 4. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 5. Mix well and serve.

Beef Stir-Fry

A flavorful beef stir-fry with broccoli and bell peppers.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g beef strips
  • - 1 cup broccoli florets
  • - 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • 2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  • 3. Add beef strips and cook until browned.
  • 4. Add broccoli and bell peppers to the pan.
  • 5. Pour soy sauce over the mixture and stir-fry until vegetables are tender.
  • 6. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 7. Serve warm.

Salmon and Quinoa Bowl

A nutritious bowl with baked salmon, quinoa, and fresh vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g salmon fillet
  • - 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • - 1/4 cup cucumbers, sliced
  • - 1/4 cup red onions, sliced
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 lemon, juiced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • 2. Place salmon fillet on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
  • 4. In a bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions.
  • 5. Top with baked salmon.
  • 6. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve.
Chicken and Veggie Skewers

Flavorful skewers with marinated chicken and colorful vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g chicken breast, cubed
  • - 1 cup bell peppers, cubed
  • - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • - 1/4 cup red onions, cubed
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  • 2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  • 3. Marinate chicken cubes for at least 30 minutes.
  • 4. Thread chicken and vegetables onto skewers.
  • 5. Grill skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until chicken is cooked through.
  • 6. Serve warm.

Chicken and Rice Bowl

A satisfying bowl with seasoned chicken, brown rice, and vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g chicken breast, sliced
  • - 1 cup brown rice, cooked
  • - 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • - 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • - 1/4 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook brown rice according to package instructions.
  • 2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  • 3. Add chicken slices and cook until fully cooked.
  • 4. Add bell peppers, corn, and black beans.
  • 5. Pour soy sauce over the mixture and stir well.
  • 6. Serve chicken and veggies over cooked brown rice.
  • 7. Season with salt and pepper.
