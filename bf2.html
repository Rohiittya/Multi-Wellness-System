Muscle Gain Breakfast Recipes

Protein Pancakes

Fluffy protein pancakes to kickstart your day with a protein boost.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup protein powder
  • - 1 cup oats
  • - 1 cup cottage cheese
  • - 2 eggs
  • - 1 tsp baking powder
  • - 1/2 cup almond milk
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  • 2. Cook on a non-stick pan until golden brown.
  • 3. Serve with fresh berries and honey.
Smoothie Bowl

A thick smoothie bowl topped with nuts and fruits for extra protein and energy.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 banana
  • - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • - 1/2 cup almond milk
  • - 1 tbsp protein powder
  • - 1/4 cup mixed nuts
  • - 1/4 cup sliced fruits
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Blend the banana, yogurt, almond milk, and protein powder.
  • 2. Pour into a bowl and top with mixed nuts and sliced fruits.
  • 3. Enjoy your protein-packed smoothie bowl!
Egg Whites and Veggies

A simple, protein-rich breakfast with egg whites and fresh vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 4 egg whites
  • - 1/2 cup spinach
  • - 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • - 1/4 cup onions, diced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Sauté the vegetables in a non-stick pan.
  • 2. Add the egg whites and cook until done.
  • 3. Season with salt and pepper.
Greek Yogurt Parfait

A layered Greek yogurt parfait with granola and fresh berries.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • - 1/2 cup granola
  • - 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • - 1 tbsp honey
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Layer the yogurt, granola, and berries in a glass.
  • 2. Drizzle with honey.
  • 3. Repeat the layers as desired.
Avocado Toast

A classic avocado toast with a twist of lemon and chili flakes.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 ripe avocado
  • - 2 slices whole grain bread
  • - 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • - Salt, pepper, and chili flakes to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Toast the bread to your liking.
  • 2. Mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Spread the avocado mixture on the toasted bread.
  • 4. Sprinkle with chili flakes.
Quinoa Salad

A refreshing quinoa salad with mixed vegetables and a lemon vinaigrette.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • - 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • - 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Mix the quinoa and vegetables in a bowl.
  • 2. Whisk the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  • 3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
Chickpea Stir-Fry

A quick and flavorful chickpea stir-fry with veggies and soy sauce.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • - 1 cup broccoli florets
  • - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • - 1 carrot, julienned
  • - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 clove garlic, minced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat the olive oil in a pan and sauté the garlic.
  • 2. Add the chickpeas and vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  • 3. Add the soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  • 4. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, then serve.
Lentil Soup

A hearty lentil soup with vegetables and spices.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • - 1 onion, diced
  • - 2 carrots, diced
  • - 2 celery stalks, diced
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 can diced tomatoes
  • - 4 cups vegetable broth
  • - 1 tsp cumin
  • - 1 tsp paprika
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic in a pot.
  • 2. Add the lentils, tomatoes, broth, and spices.
  • 3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30-40 minutes until lentils are tender.
  • 4. Season with salt and pepper before serving.
Grilled Chicken Salad

A healthy grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and a balsamic vinaigrette.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • - 2 cups mixed greens
  • - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • - 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • - 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • - 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Arrange the mixed greens on a plate.
  • 2. Top with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and sliced grilled chicken.
  • 3. Whisk the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  • 4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad before serving.
Chocolate Banana Smoothie

A delicious chocolate banana smoothie perfect for a quick breakfast or snack.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 ripe banana
  • - 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • - 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • - 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • - A handful of ice cubes
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  • 2. Blend until smooth.
  • 3. Pour into a glass and enjoy.
