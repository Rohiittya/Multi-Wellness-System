Gluten-Free Weight Maintenance Recipes

Quinoa and Black Bean Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked and drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions using water or vegetable broth.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, black beans, red bell pepper, and red onion.
  3. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice. Toss gently to combine.

Herb-Crusted Tofu

Ingredients:
  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place tofu cubes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a small bowl, combine gluten-free breadcrumbs, parsley, thyme, and lemon zest.
  5. Press the breadcrumb mixture onto the tofu cubes.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the tofu is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve with lemon wedges.

Greek Chickpea Patties

Ingredients:
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 1/4 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine mashed chickpeas, gluten-free breadcrumbs, feta cheese, minced garlic, parsley, dill, egg, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix well and form into patties.
  4. Place the patties on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the patties are golden brown.
  6. Serve with a side of tzatziki sauce if desired.

Avocado and Edamame Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup edamame, shelled and cooked
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Procedure:
  1. In a large bowl, combine edamame, diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lime juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 3 tbsp gluten-free soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1/2 tsp ginger, minced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds for garnish
Procedure:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced ginger and garlic, and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together gluten-free soy sauce and hoisin sauce.
  5. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.

Cauliflower Fried Rice

Ingredients:
  • 1 head cauliflower, grated or riced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tbsp gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Procedure:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add riced cauliflower and cook for 5-7 minutes or until tender.
  4. Push the cauliflower to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked through, then combine with the cauliflower.
  5. Add peas and carrots, gluten-free soy sauce, sesame oil, and sliced green onions. Stir to combine and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
