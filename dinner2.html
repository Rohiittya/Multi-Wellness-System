Baked Salmon with Asparagus

A nutritious dinner with baked salmon and roasted asparagus.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g salmon fillet
  • - 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 lemon, sliced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • 2. Place salmon fillet on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Arrange asparagus around the salmon.
  • 4. Top with lemon slices.
  • 5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until salmon is cooked through and asparagus is tender.
  • 6. Serve warm.
Chicken Quinoa Salad

A hearty salad with grilled chicken, quinoa, and fresh vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g grilled chicken breast
  • - 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • - 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • - 1/4 cup red onions, sliced
  • - 1/4 cup cucumbers, sliced
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Grill the chicken breast and slice into strips.
  • 2. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions.
  • 3. Mix the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onions, and cucumbers in a bowl.
  • 4. Top with grilled chicken and cooked quinoa.
  • 5. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  • 6. Season with salt and pepper.
Stuffed Bell Peppers

Bell peppers stuffed with a flavorful mixture of ground beef and vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 4 bell peppers, tops cut off and seeds removed
  • - 200g ground beef
  • - 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • - 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • - 1/4 cup onions, diced
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • 2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  • 3. Add onions and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  • 4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  • 5. Mix in cooked rice, diced tomatoes, salt, and pepper.
  • 6. Stuff the bell peppers with the mixture.
  • 7. Place stuffed peppers in a baking dish, sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  • 8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until peppers are tender and cheese is melted.
Turkey Meatballs

Delicious turkey meatballs served with marinara sauce and whole wheat pasta.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g ground turkey
  • - 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • - 1 egg, beaten
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 jar marinara sauce
  • - 200g whole wheat pasta
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • 2. In a bowl, mix ground turkey, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, beaten egg, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Form the mixture into meatballs.
  • 4. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and brown the meatballs on all sides.
  • 5. Transfer meatballs to a baking dish and pour marinara sauce over them.
  • 6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until meatballs are cooked through.
  • 7. Cook whole wheat pasta according to package instructions.
  • 8. Serve meatballs and marinara sauce.
  • 7. Cook whole wheat pasta according to package instructions.
  • 8. Serve meatballs and marinara sauce over the cooked pasta.
Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Tasty shrimp skewers grilled to perfection with a zesty marinade.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • - 1 lemon, juiced
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tsp paprika
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, mix lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  • 2. Marinate shrimp for at least 30 minutes.
  • 3. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  • 4. Thread marinated shrimp onto skewers.
  • 5. Grill shrimp skewers for 3-4 minutes per side or until shrimp is opaque and cooked through.
  • 6. Serve warm.
Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu

A colorful stir-fry with a mix of vegetables and tofu.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup broccoli florets
  • - 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • - 1/2 cup snap peas
  • - 200g tofu, cubed
  • - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • 2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  • 3. Add tofu and cook until golden brown.
  • 4. Add broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas.
  • 5. Pour soy sauce over the mixture and stir-fry until vegetables are tender.
  • 6. Season with salt and pepper.
  • 7. Serve warm.

Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato

A balanced meal with grilled chicken breast and roasted sweet potatoes.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 200g chicken breast
  • - 2 medium sweet potatoes, cubed
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tsp paprika
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • 2. Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Spread sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender.
  • 4. Meanwhile, season chicken breast with salt and pepper.
  • 5. Grill the chicken over medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  • 6. Serve the grilled chicken with roasted sweet potatoes.
