Overall Wellness Dinner Recipes

Chicken Fajitas

A flavorful and protein-packed dinner with tender chicken, bell peppers, and onions, served with warm tortillas.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • - 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • - 1 onion, sliced
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp fajita seasoning
  • - 4 flour tortillas
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • 2. Add chicken, bell peppers, and onion, and cook until the chicken is fully cooked and vegetables are tender.
  • 3. Add fajita seasoning and mix well.
  • 4. Serve in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings like guacamole, salsa, and sour cream.
Grilled Salmon with Veggies

A healthy and easy-to-make dinner with grilled salmon, served with a side of roasted vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 salmon fillets
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 lemon, sliced
  • - 1 cup asparagus, trimmed
  • - 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  • 2. Drizzle olive oil over the salmon fillets and season with salt, pepper, and lemon slices.
  • 3. Grill the salmon for about 6-8 minutes on each side.
  • 4. Roast asparagus and cherry tomatoes on the grill or in the oven.
  • 5. Serve the grilled salmon with the roasted veggies on the side.
Vegetarian Lentil Soup

A wholesome and hearty soup made with lentils, tomatoes, carrots, and celery—perfect for a filling dinner.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup dried lentils
  • - 1 onion, chopped
  • - 2 carrots, chopped
  • - 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • - 1 can diced tomatoes
  • - 4 cups vegetable broth
  • - 1 tsp cumin
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a large pot, sauté onions, carrots, and celery until softened.
  • 2. Add the lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for about 30 minutes or until lentils are tender.
  • 4. Serve hot with a slice of whole grain bread for extra heartiness.
Chickpea Curry

A quick and easy vegetarian curry made with chickpeas, tomatoes, and coconut milk, perfect for a flavorful dinner.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • - 1 can coconut milk
  • - 1 onion, chopped
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 tbsp curry powder
  • - 1 can diced tomatoes
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat olive oil in a large pot and sauté onions and garlic until softened.
  • 2. Add curry powder and cook for another minute.
  • 3. Stir in the chickpeas, coconut milk, and diced tomatoes.
  • 4. Simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • 5. Serve with steamed rice or naan bread for a complete meal.
Spaghetti and Meatballs

A classic dinner dish of spaghetti with homemade meatballs, served with marinara sauce.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • - 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • - 1 egg
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 cup marinara sauce
  • - 1 package spaghetti
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • 2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Shape into meatballs and bake for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  • 4. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions, and heat marinara sauce in a pan.
  • 5. Serve the meatballs on top of spaghetti with marinara sauce.
Teriyaki Chicken

A sweet and savory dish with tender chicken, cooked in a delicious homemade teriyaki sauce.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • - 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • - 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • - 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 tbsp cornstarch (optional for thicker sauce)
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, and garlic to make the teriyaki sauce.
  • 2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides.
  • 3. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken and let it simmer until the sauce thickens and the chicken is fully cooked.
  • 4. Serve with steamed rice and vegetables.
Rotisserie Chicken Tacos

Quick and easy tacos made with rotisserie chicken, perfect for a busy night.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 cups rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • - 8 small corn tortillas
  • - 1/2 cup salsa
  • - 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • - 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • - 1/4 cup sour cream
  • - Lime wedges for serving
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for 1-2 minutes on each side.
  • 2. Warm the shredded rotisserie chicken with salsa in a pan.
  • 3. Assemble tacos by adding the chicken, lettuce, cheese, and sour cream to each tortilla.
  • 4. Serve with lime wedges for an extra burst of flavor.
Vegetarian Burrito Bowl

A healthy and customizable bowl filled with rice, beans, veggies, and your favorite toppings.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • - 1 cup black beans, cooked
  • - 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • - 1 avocado, sliced
  • - 1/2 cup salsa
  • - 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • - Lime wedges for serving
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Layer the cooked brown rice in the bottom of a bowl.
  • 2. Add black beans, corn, and salsa on top of the rice.
  • 3. Top with sliced avocado, shredded cheese, and any other favorite toppings.
  • 4. Serve with a squeeze of lime juice for added freshness.
Smothered Balsamic Chicken

A savory and tangy chicken dish cooked in a balsamic glaze, perfect with mashed potatoes or rice.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • - 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp honey
  • - 1 tsp garlic powder
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook chicken breasts until golden and cooked through.
  • 2. In a bowl, mix balsamic vinegar, honey, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Pour the balsamic mixture over the chicken and cook for an additional 3-5 minutes, letting it thicken.
  • 4. Serve hot with mashed potatoes or rice.
Creamy Chicken Pasta

A rich and creamy pasta with succulent chicken breast, garlic, and Parmesan cheese.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced
  • - 8 oz pasta of your choice
  • - 1 cup heavy cream
  • - 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook pasta according to package instructions and set aside.
  • 2. In a pan, sauté garlic until fragrant, then add heavy cream and Parmesan cheese.
  • 3. Stir the sauce until it thickens, then add the sliced chicken and cooked pasta.
  • 4. Toss everything together and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • 5. Serve with a sprinkle of extra Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley.
Shrimp Scampi

A delicious and light seafood dish with shrimp in a lemon-garlic butter sauce, served over pasta.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • - 8 oz pasta
  • - 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1/4 cup white wine
  • - 1/4 cup butter
  • - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook pasta according to package instructions, then set aside.
  • 2. In a large pan, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  • 3. Add shrimp and cook until pink, then add white wine and let it simmer for a minute.
  • 4. Add the cooked pasta and parsley, tossing to coat. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • 5. Serve with extra parsley on top for garnish.
Turkey Burgers

A healthier alternative to traditional burgers, made with ground turkey and topped with fresh veggies.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 lb ground turkey
  • - 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • - 1 egg
  • - 1 tsp garlic powder
  • - 4 whole wheat burger buns
  • - Lettuce, tomato, and other preferred toppings
  • Procedure:
  • 1. In a bowl, mix the ground turkey, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, egg, and garlic powder.
  • 2. Form the mixture into burger patties.
  • 3. Cook the patties on a grill or in a pan over medium heat until fully cooked.
  • 4. Serve on whole wheat buns with your favorite burger toppings.
