Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:
- 200g grilled chicken breast
- Mixed salad greens
- Cherry tomatoes
- Cucumber
- Olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.
2. Chop the vegetables and mix them with the salad greens.
3. Slice the grilled chicken and add to the salad.
4. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:
- 4 bell peppers
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 can black beans, drained
- Corn kernels
- Diced tomatoes
- Chopped cilantro
- Cumin
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove seeds.
3. In a bowl, mix cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, tomatoes, and cilantro.
4. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper.
5. Stuff the bell peppers with the quinoa mixture.
6. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Baked Salmon with Asparagus

Ingredients:
- 2 salmon fillets
- 1 bunch asparagus
- Lemon slices
- Olive oil
- Garlic powder
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Place salmon fillets and asparagus on a baking sheet.
3. Drizzle olive oil over the salmon and asparagus.
4. Season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
5. Top the salmon with lemon slices.
6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the salmon is cooked through.

Cauliflower Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- 1 head cauliflower, grated
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
- 2 eggs, beaten
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Green onions
- Garlic
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add grated cauliflower and cook for 5 minutes until tender.
3. Push cauliflower to the side and pour beaten eggs into the pan. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked.
4. Mix the scrambled eggs with the cauliflower.
5. Add mixed vegetables and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
6. Add soy sauce and cook for an additional minute.
7. Stir in chopped green onions and minced garlic.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients:
- Sweet potatoes
- Black beans
- Corn
- Avocado
- Salsa
- Cilantro
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Bake the sweet potatoes until tender.

4. Fill with black beans, corn, avocado, and salsa.
5. Garnish with cilantro.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas, carrots)
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Heat sesame oil in a pan.
2. Add garlic and ginger, then the mixed vegetables.
3. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender.
4. Add soy sauce and cook for another minute.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients:
- Eggplant slices
- Tomato sauce
- Mozzarella cheese
- Parmesan cheese
- Bread crumbs
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Bread the eggplant slices with bread crumbs.
3. Fry the slices until golden brown.
4. Layer the eggplant, tomato sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Tofu Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- Tofu, cubed
- Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas)
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Salt and pepper

Procedure:
1. Heat sesame oil in a pan.
2. Add garlic and ginger, then the tofu cubes.
3. Stir-fry until the tofu is golden brown.
4. Add the mixed vegetables and soy sauce.
5. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
