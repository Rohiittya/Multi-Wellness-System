Overall Wellness Lunch Recipes

Grilled Chicken Salad

A light and healthy salad with grilled chicken, mixed greens, and a lemon vinaigrette.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 chicken breast, grilled
  • - 2 cups mixed greens
  • - 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • - 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - Juice of 1 lemon
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Grill the chicken breast and slice it thinly.
  • 2. Toss mixed greens, cucumber, and avocado in a bowl.
  • 3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  • 4. Season with salt and pepper and top with grilled chicken.
Vegetarian Buddha Bowl

A nourishing and colorful bowl filled with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and hummus.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1/2 cup quinoa, cooked
  • - 1/2 cup roasted sweet potatoes
  • - 1/4 cup chickpeas, roasted
  • - 2 tbsp hummus
  • - 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • - A handful of spinach
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  • 2. Roast sweet potatoes and chickpeas until crispy.
  • 3. In a bowl, layer quinoa, roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and spinach.
  • 4. Top with hummus and avocado slices.
Grilled Salmon Sandwich

A wholesome sandwich with grilled salmon, spinach, and a whole grain bun.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 salmon fillet, grilled
  • - 2 whole grain sandwich buns
  • - A handful of spinach
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Grill the salmon fillet and season with salt and pepper.
  • 2. Toast the whole grain buns.
  • 3. Layer spinach and the grilled salmon on the bun.
  • 4. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil.
Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken

A protein-packed salad with quinoa, grilled chicken, and a refreshing lemon dressing.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1/2 cup quinoa, cooked
  • - 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • - 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • - 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - Juice of 1 lemon
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook quinoa and let it cool.
  • 2. Grill the chicken and slice it thinly.
  • 3. Toss quinoa, cucumber, red onion, and chicken together in a bowl.
  • 4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, season with salt and pepper.
Chickpea Burger

A plant-based chickpea patty served with veggies on a whole grain bun.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • - 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • - 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1/2 tsp cumin
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Mash the chickpeas in a bowl.
  • 2. Add breadcrumbs, chopped onion, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Form into patties and cook in olive oil until golden brown.
  • 4. Serve on whole grain buns with lettuce and tomato.
Vegan Pasta Salad

A fresh pasta salad made with roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and a tangy vinaigrette.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup cooked pasta (your choice)
  • - 1/2 cup chickpeas, roasted
  • - 1 cup roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, etc.)
  • - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Roast the vegetables and chickpeas in the oven with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • 2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  • 3. Toss the pasta with roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and balsamic vinegar.
  • 4. Season with salt and pepper and serve chilled or at room temperature.
Lentil Soup

A hearty and warming lentil soup with vegetables and a rich broth.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup dried lentils
  • - 1 carrot, diced
  • - 1 onion, chopped
  • - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • - 4 cups vegetable broth
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat olive oil in a large pot, add onions, carrots, and garlic. Sauté until softened.
  • 2. Add lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30-40 minutes until lentils are tender.
  • 3. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.
Vegetarian Sushi Rolls

Delicious sushi rolls filled with avocado, cucumber, and other fresh vegetables.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup sushi rice
  • - 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • - 1 avocado, sliced
  • - 1 cucumber, julienned
  • - 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • - Soy sauce for dipping
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook sushi rice and season with rice vinegar.
  • 2. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat and spread a thin layer of rice on top.
  • 3. Place avocado and cucumber slices along the edge of the rice.
  • 4. Roll the sushi tightly, slice into bite-sized pieces, and serve with soy sauce.
Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

A flavorful and nutritious stuffed chicken breast with spinach, feta cheese, and herbs.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 chicken breasts
  • - 1/2 cup spinach, sautéed
  • - 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Slice a pocket into the chicken breasts and stuff with sautéed spinach and crumbled feta.
  • 2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook the chicken until golden brown and cooked through.

Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy stir-fry with chicken, colorful vegetables, and a soy-based sauce.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • - 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • - 1 zucchini, sliced
  • - 1 onion, sliced
  • - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté chicken until browned.
  • 2. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  • 3. Add soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  • 4. Serve with brown rice or noodles.
Spaghetti with Tomato and Basil

A simple and satisfying pasta dish with fresh tomatoes, basil, and garlic.

  • Ingredients:
  • - 1 cup spaghetti
  • - 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • - 1 garlic clove, minced
  • - Fresh basil leaves
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • Procedure:
  • 1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  • 2. Sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  • 3. Add tomatoes and cook until softened.
  • 4. Toss cooked spaghetti with tomato mixture and fresh basil.
  • 5. Season with salt and pepper and serve warm.
