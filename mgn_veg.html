Muscle-Gain Non-Veg Recipes

1. Grilled Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill on medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
  3. Serve with steamed vegetables or brown rice.

2. Salmon with Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the salmon and asparagus on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Top the salmon with lemon slices and bake for 15-20 minutes.

3. Turkey Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Procedure:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the ground turkey and cook until browned.
  2. Add the garlic, bell pepper, and broccoli. Cook until tender.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce, toss, and serve.

4. Shrimp and Quinoa Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  2. Combine cooked quinoa, shrimp, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with lemon juice, season with salt and pepper, and serve.

5. Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

Procedure:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a skillet over high heat. Add beef and cook until browned.
  2. Add garlic and broccoli, and stir-fry until broccoli is tender.
  3. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce, toss, and serve.

6. Egg and Turkey Breakfast Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 3 slices turkey breast
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Scramble the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Layer the eggs, turkey, and avocado on the tortilla. Wrap tightly and serve.

7. Grilled Tuna Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 tuna steaks
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Brush tuna steaks with olive oil and season with garlic powder, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill on high heat for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  3. Serve with a side of grilled vegetables or salad.

8. Chicken and Sweet Potato Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken thighs
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss diced sweet potatoes with olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Grill the chicken thighs until fully cooked. Serve on top of roasted sweet potatoes.

9. Baked Cod with Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 cod fillets
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the cod and sliced vegetables on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the cod is flaky.

10. Turkey Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and form into small meatballs.
  3. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes.

11. Grilled Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Rub lamb chops with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill on medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. Serve with a side of mashed sweet potatoes.

12. Egg and Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 chicken breast, grilled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Toss until evenly mixed. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
